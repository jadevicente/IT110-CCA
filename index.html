About Us

G2Convert4r was created with a clear purpose and that is to provide a reliable, easy-to-use currency conversion tool for everyone, especially Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs) and travelers who need quick and accurate exchange rate information. We understand that staying informed about currency values is essential for making smart financial decisions when working abroad or planning international trips.

As third-year BSIT students at Caraga State University, Ampayon, we developed this application as our midterm project for IT 110. Our goal was to combine our technical skills with a real-world solution that addresses the practical needs of our fellow Filipinos working overseas and anyone engaging in international transactions.
